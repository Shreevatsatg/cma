ABOUT MGM:
Mahatma Gandhi Memorial College ,Udupi, founded in the year 1949 by Dr.tonse Madhava Ananth Pai, was his dreamchild, visulaised to bring education to the doors of the youth of udupi and its neighbouring towns. Until then, there were only two colleges in the dakshina karnataka .the college celebrated its Silver Jubliee,Golden Jubliee and the Daimond Jubliee with great splender. We can recall with pride the milstones in the path of the development of the college including the inuagratiion of Madhava Raksha.
